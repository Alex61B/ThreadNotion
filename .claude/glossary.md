# ThreadNotion Glossary

**Simulation** — A single roleplay conversation between a user and an AI persona, conducted in one of four modes (generic, adaptive, drill, mixed_practice). Stored as a `Conversation` record with associated `Message` rows.

**Evaluation summary** — The post-conversation feedback record stored in `SimulationEvaluationSummary`. Contains deterministic transcript metrics, LLM-scored skill breakdowns, weakness flags, recommended tips, and the raw LLM output for debugging.

**Weakness profile** — A per-user, per-skill record (`UserWeaknessProfile`) tracking current score and trend direction across simulations. Updated after every evaluated conversation.

**Progress snapshot** — The aggregated view of a user's skill scores and trends over time, surfaced in progress/dashboard views. Derived from `SimulationSkillScore` history and weakness profiles.

**Adaptive scenario plan** — A JSON blob stored on `Conversation.adaptiveScenarioPlan` at conversation creation. Encodes the targeted weakness and scenario parameters for an adaptive-mode session.

**Drill plan** — A JSON blob stored on `Conversation.drillPlan` at conversation creation. Encodes the focused skill and exercise structure for a drill-mode session.

**Training recommendation** — A suggested next action (mode, skill focus, or assignment) generated by `src/services/trainingRecommendationService.ts` based on weakness profile and recent session history.

**Org admin** — A user with `AccountRole.MANAGER` who owns a `Team` and can manage `TeamMember` records and `TrainingAssignment` rows for their team.

**Team seat** — A `TeamMember` row linking a userId to a `Team`. Seat count is governed by `Entitlement.maxSeats` on the team's billing account.

**Roleplay session** — Synonym for simulation; used in UI copy and product context to describe the user-facing experience of a conversation with an AI persona.
